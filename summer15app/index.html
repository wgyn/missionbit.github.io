Mission Bit Summer 2015 Mobile App/Game Development Course

Please complete and submit the form below. The first class meeting is Monday June 1st from 9am-4pm.


Mission Bit is offering a 6 week immersive mobile app/game development course this summer. The class will focus on the entire 'first alpha' mobile app/game development process from ideation to 'alpha demo'. We are looking for students passionate about mobile app/game development and willing to commit 6 weeks of the summer to the class. The class will be working together as a single 'mock product group' so active engaged participation every day and for the entire 6 week period is required. The course is designed for students with some coding experience. At the end of the class, the students will present their app/game to industry insiders to judge whether their app/game should be green lit. The class will be taught, supported and mentored by professional software engineers. Leading companies in the mobile app/game industry will support by sharing best practices and insights through out the class' process of developing their mobile app/game. Participating companies include Tencent, DeNA, Chartboost. Other exciting participating companies will be announced soon.

Students entering high school in the Fall 2015, current high school students and college freshman and sophomores are welcome to signup/apply for the class.

Class Details
* Class Size: between 6-12
* Class Period: June 1 - July 10 (no class on July 3rd and July 4th)
* Schedule: The class will meet Monday through Friday from 9am - 4pm
* Location: TBD
* Transportation: Students are responsible for their own transportation to and from the class. Mission Bit will provide transportation for special field trips.
* Food: Mission Bit will provide dry breakfast items (e.g. fruit and bread) and afternoon snacks. Students are responsible for their own lunch.
* Computers and materials: Mission Bit will provide computers and all other necessary materials for the class.
Please send us an email at info@missionbit.com with "Summer Mobile Class" in the subject if you have questions and/or comments. Look forward to seeing you this summer. This class will be great!
